    The Professional Shadow Program is a practical training initiative organized by the Sharjah Governmental Media Bureau to train university students. The program aims to give participants hands-on experience with the workflows, regulations, and systems in place across the Media Bureau. It offers trainees the opportunity to mimic real-life organizations, where each department has its own tasks and responsibilities, and participants learn how to communicate and collaborate with other virtual departments to complete projects. In addition, the program includes specialized workshops organized by the Training Department and delivered by professionals from each department of the Bureau.
